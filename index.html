MY JOURNEY

From Late Night Bars to Global Dreams

My journey as BONITAKLISH began when I was just 15 years old — I fell in love with making music, writing my first bars in my room late at night, dreaming of bigger stages. Those early days were raw and unfiltered, filled with notebooks, voice memos, and an unshakeable belief that my words could move people.

From those early freestyles to now, I've grown not just as an artist, but as a man with a vision: to inspire, move, and uplift through my rap. Every song is a piece of my story, every verse a chapter in a journey that's still being written. The struggles, the late nights, the moments of doubt — they've all shaped the artist you hear today.

My dream is to one day reach listeners all over the world, to tell my story, and to prove that anything is possible when you commit to the craft. This isn't just about music — it's about legacy, connection, and showing the next generation that their dreams are valid. Every track I drop, every performance I give, is a step closer to that vision.
